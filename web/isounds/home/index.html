iSounds is up in BigBoss repo!


iSounds is a tweak to enrich your iOS experience. It will add sounds to the user interface, just like in Android, but for far more things than in Android. Opening an app, navigating through menus, tapping the switches, opening folders, sliding sliders, watching the progress bars slide, locking, unlocking, restarting your phone — everything will be done with with sounds from your favorite game, OS, movie, app, or whatever! It’s up to you, and if you use it with a WinterBoard theme that matches the soundset, it gives you a great experience!
So, why are you still waiting? Get it while you can, because it’s what makes iPhone an iPhone!
